Somebodies bunny

I was looking at plants at the Portland Nursery when a small soft bunny walked up to me and let me hold it. I waited for 30 minutes and no one came for the bunny even though it was clearly domesticated. I wrapped it up in my jacket and ran into my car, having decided that they forfeited clover after 30 minutes of me waiting.



Now clover is pooping everywhere. I don't know how to raise a bunny. That is the story of the last 24 hours of clovers life. Good luck clover.
